Reverse Hand pushups

How to: Start in standard push-up position. Slowly rotate your arms outward until your fingers are pointed toward your toes. Move your hands a few inches toward your feet into a comfortable position, but keep your arms straight. Engage your core. Slowly lower yourself toward the ground. Your wrists and forearms might not be flexible enough yet, so don't force yourself to go lower than what feels comfortable. Hold the position for two seconds, then push yourself up.
