Duck walks

How to:Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, and cross hands in front of chest. Slowly sit hips back and down into a half squat position. Keeping hips stable, lower right knee down to the ground, followed by left. Bring right foot forward, followed by left, and return to half-squat position. That’s 1 rep.
