Svend Press

How to: Hold one or two small plates (5's or 10's) sandwiched together between your palms at chest height. Raise your elbows so your arms are parallel to the floor and your fingers are forward. Move: Extend your arms and press the plates straight out from your chest, then slowly return to the start.
