Standing Dumbbell upward fly

How to: Stand with feet hip width apart, Grab dumbbells with an underhand grip Lift the dumbbells up to shoulder height by contracting your chest until the weights meet at the middle of your body, Slowly lower to starting position. Repeat desired reps.
