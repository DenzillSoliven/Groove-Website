Flutter kicks

How to: Place both your hands underneath your buttocks. Keep your lower back on the ground as you lift the right leg off the ground slightly past hip height, and lift the left leg so it hovers a few inches off the floor. Hold for 2 seconds, then switch the position of the legs, making a flutter kick motion.
