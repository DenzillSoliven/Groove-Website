Assisted one arm pushups

How to:Start in standard push-up position but use your secondary arm to assist your pushing arm by reaching it out to the side and/or placing it on a slightly elevated surface. The idea is to rely as little as possible on the assisting arm and do as much of the work as possible with your primary arm. Over time, you will rely on it less and less until you eventually don't need it at all.
