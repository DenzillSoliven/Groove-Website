Cross body curls

How to:Begin by standing up straight with your feet shoulder-width apart and arms by your side, with a dumbbell in each hand. Face your palms toward your body and do a cross-body weight draw with a curl of your right hand from your right hip to your right shoulder, and then left shoulder. Alternate arms for next repetitions.
